Classic Caprese Salad

Introduction

Indulge in the timeless elegance of Italian cuisine with our Classic Caprese Salad recipe. This vibrant dish celebrates the harmony of simple, high-quality ingredients, bringing together juicy ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and aromatic basil leaves. Each bite is a symphony of flavors and textures, enhanced by a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and, if desired, a touch of balsamic glaze. Whether served as a refreshing appetizer, a light lunch, or a side dish to accompany your favorite meal, our Caprese Salad promises to delight the senses and transport you to the sun-kissed shores of the Mediterranean. Gather your freshest produce, embrace the simplicity of Italian tradition, and prepare to savor the deliciousness of our Classic Caprese Salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 ball fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a serving plate, overlapping slightly.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves in between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the salad.
  4. Optionally, drizzle balsamic glaze over the salad for added sweetness and flavor.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.
